Client → Opportunity → Delivery System

Operations Manager Performance Task

Services & AI Platform | California Public Sector

Designing a scalable system for lead-to-delivery with trust preservation

Objective

Design a system that:

  • Maintains shared understanding of each client
  • Guides services expansion and AI product adoption
  • Moves smoothly: Lead → Opportunity → Contract → Delivery
  • Preserves trust across sales, operations, and delivery
Realistic to implement in 3–6 months, no major restructuring

Part 1: Client Account Profile

Five sections — mandatory vs optional, continuous vs periodic updates

Mandatory

  • Client Overview
  • Current Engagement

Optional (as needed)

  • Systems & Constraints
  • AI Opportunity Signals
  • Decision History

Part 2: Lead to Delivery Flow

Lead/Account→ Opportunity→ Proposal→ Contract→ Delivery Kickoff

Each stage: purpose, entry criteria, owner, contributors, approval point

Handoffs: Sales → Ops at qualification; Ops → Delivery at contract

Part 3: Guardrails

  • Services first for new relationships
  • Product readiness — 6+ months engagement, use case documented
  • Pilot before scale — 3–6 month pilots unless prior success
  • Handoff checklist — capacity confirmed before contract
  • Trust preservation — never undermine services trust

Part 5 & 6: Feedback & Incentives

Feedback Loops

  • Delivery → Sales (weekly sync, milestones, risks)
  • Profile enrichment (every touch, quarterly review)

Incentive Alignment

Tension: Sales speed vs delivery readiness

Mechanism: Handoff checklist + metric (proposal→kickoff time) + policy (commission tied to kickoff)
